Biography

  • Xu Tang received the B.S. degrees in Hefei University of Technology and M.S. degrees in University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2014 and 2017 respectively. He has been working on computer vision in Baidu, Inc. since 2017. He is now a Senior R&D Engineer of computer vision team in Baidu, Inc. His research interests focus on face detection and recognition, tracking.
  • 汤旭，百度资深研发工程师，百度人脸检测负责人，主要负责检测相关算法优化、模型加速、服务端sdk、移动端sdk、paddle框架迁移等工作。主要研究人脸检测、通用检测和目标跟踪，在CVPR、ECCV、TIFS等权威国际会议和期刊发表论文。多次刷新Widerface国际榜单记录，在权威的ICCV Wider Challenge人脸检测竞赛、ICCV VOT2019单目标跟踪竞赛获得冠军。担任ICCV、AAAI、Neurocomputing、CVPR等国际会议和期刊审稿人，曾获百度AIG-TC技术先锋奖和人脸技术部季度之星。
